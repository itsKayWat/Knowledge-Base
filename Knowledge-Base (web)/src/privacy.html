Privacy Policy for Knowledge Base

Effective Date: May 22, 2025

Version: 1.0.0

Introduction

Knowledge Base is a browser extension designed to provide a powerful system for organizing, managing, and accessing your information. It enables you to create customized collections, upload files, write articles, and build a searchable repository of knowledge all within your browser.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users. All your content and settings are stored locally on your device using the browser's storage API. No data is transmitted to external servers unless you explicitly choose to export your data for backup purposes.

Data Usage

All data created within the Knowledge Base extension (including articles, files, categories, and settings) remains on your device. Your information is accessible only to you and is not used for any purposes beyond providing the extension's functionality.

Local Storage

Knowledge Base uses your browser's built-in storage API to save:

  • Content created within the extension (articles, categories, files)
  • User preferences and settings
  • Organizational structure of books and categories
  • File data uploaded by users

This data is stored locally on your device and is not accessible to other extensions or websites.

Data Sharing

We do not share any user data with third parties. Your content remains private and under your control at all times.

Data Export & Backup

Knowledge Base allows you to export your data for backup purposes. This exported data remains under your control, and the extension does not monitor or track how you use these exports.

User Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you maintain complete control over your information. You can delete any or all content at any time through the extension's interface.

Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@knowledgebase-extension.com.
